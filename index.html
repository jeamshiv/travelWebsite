About Us

The world is a place of captivating encounters. It is vast and it is mysterious with so much to offer. Our job is to curate these bespoke and amazing adventures for you across continents- to bring the remarkable to you.

Outlandish Journeys was created for the love of discovering new, far away places, people and cultures you never knew existed. These experiences will be revealed to you by the very people who know every facet of your journey. So while you find yourself amidst idyllic archipelagos, unforgettable landscapes, mesmerising horizons and greener pastures, you will be forging connections with vibrant cultures and warm hospitable communities.

We are a company that set high standards for ourselves and so we offer you the very best, without compromising the integrity of the environment and people that we work with.

We are Outlandish Journeys!

With regards,

Amit Bawa
